## Create New Device

+ Go to the "MY Devices" option and click "New Device":![new Device](./img/newDevice.png)

+ Choose the  "From Template" option. Select  "Quickstart Template " and enter "SensePi" as the Device Name. ![Template](./img/newDevice2.png)

+ Click "Create" and you should see the default Dashboard for your Device:
  ![Dashboard](./img/dash1.png)

+ Leave this page open in the browser, you'll need it in the next step...
